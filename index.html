about us

Lianna Barbu is the Principal of OnPoint Solutions, a consulting firm specializing in performance management, process improvement, grants management, and federal compliance for non-profit and governmental agencies. Previously, Lianna served as Chief Operating Officer of Community Shelter Board, where she led internal operations, overseeing finance, grants, housing, IT, and HR. She directed the organization’s data, research, and evaluation efforts, providing critical insights for strategic decision-making. During her 17-year tenure, Lianna played a pivotal leadership role in the Continuum of Care for Columbus and Franklin County, driving strategic initiatives aligned with local and federal goals to address homelessness. She successfully secured the HUD Unified Funding Agency designation for CSB, a mark of excellence in federal grants management. Under her leadership, CSB expanded its annual budget to $60 million and the team to 40 staff members, fostering strategic partnerships and implementing operational best practices. Lianna holds a Master of Business Administration from The Ohio State University Fisher College of Business and a bachelor’s degree in computer science.
