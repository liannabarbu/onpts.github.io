Meet Lianna Barbu – Driving Results, Inspiring Change

Lianna Barbu is the driving force behind OnPoint Solutions, a consulting firm dedicated to empowering non-profit and government agencies to achieve transformative results. With a focus on performance management, process improvement, grants management, and federal compliance, Lianna partners with organizations to unlock their potential and create lasting impact. As the former Chief Operating Officer of the Community Shelter Board (CSB), Lianna brought vision and expertise to every aspect of the organization’s internal operations, from finance and housing to IT, HR, and grants management. Her leadership fueled strategic decision-making through cutting-edge data, research, and evaluation practices, helping CSB remain a national leader in the fight against homelessness. Over her 17-year tenure, Lianna spearheaded groundbreaking initiatives in Columbus and Franklin County, Ohio, aligning community-wide efforts with local and federal goals. Her leadership earned CSB the prestigious HUD Unified Funding Agency designation, a gold standard for federal grants management. Under her stewardship, CSB:

  • ➤ Expanded its annual budget by 75% to an impressive $60 million.
  • ➤ Grew the team by 185% to 40 staff members, fostering collaboration and operational excellence.
  • ➤ Built strategic partnerships that amplified the organization’s reach and effectiveness.

Lianna’s credentials are as robust as her impact. She holds a Master of Business Administration from The Ohio State University Fisher College of Business and a bachelor’s degree in computer science. Her unique blend of analytical expertise and visionary leadership makes her a trusted advisor for organizations seeking to elevate their performance and deliver measurable outcomes. Whether she’s guiding teams to improve processes, secure critical funding, or enhance compliance, Lianna’s mission is clear: to help organizations thrive and make a difference in the communities they serve.
